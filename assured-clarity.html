Who We Are

We are a global consultancy, specialising in Risk Management and Data Privacy, through Education, Awareness and Training, throughout an organisation. We recognise the ‘human factor’ as well as the need for advanced technology to protect and defend from a physical and or, cyber attack.

We help companies identify risks to their business; breaking down silos inside the organisation, undertaking a comprehensive risk assessment. We then work with you to design an integrated strategy for managing these risks, building trust throughout your organisation.

We are a global network of specialist advisors coming from a diverse range of industries, with wide and varied backgrounds, all with relevant and extensive experience in Risk and Resilience.

We believe that we are leaders of evolution.

Beyond our individual technical competencies, our company culture ensures we provide a positive and pragmatic approach and we deliver a safer and more resilient workplace.

GDPR

Until very recently and the European General Data Protection Regulation has been an influencer, the Information Security, Data Privacy Management and Cyber threat defence, has always been left to the IT department.

Research concludes that people within the organisation are its weakest link, with education, awareness and training being brought to the fore, we have an opportunity to create transformational change.
